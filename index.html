About Us

We make parking easier for everyone involved.

Get My Parking was founded in 2015 with the mission to digitize parking globally. We aim to make smart 
parking accessible and affordable to all businesses so that parking becomes a sub-conscious experience.

We believe in

Growing Together

Your growth is our growth - we're willing to go the extra mile so we can grow together as one big team.

Making a Difference

Do what we do to make a difference in your life and your partner's. Every minute and dollar you save is a feather on our cap.

Persistence

There's no parking problem we can't solve - we'll just keep at it until we have a solution for you.

We have fun building your solutions.

At Get My Parking, innovation and creativity are in our DNA. We believe in building our tech from 
scratch, without external acquisitions, with activities like hackathons and brain-storming sessions that 
yield powerful parking solutions. Our passionate team develops homegrown tech that makes parking 
smarter, faster, and more convenient.
